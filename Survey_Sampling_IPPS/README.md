# Survey Sampling – IPPS Designs

This project was completed as part of a Survey Sampling course at the Toulouse School of Economics.

It focuses on Inclusion Probability Proportional-to-Size (IPPS) sampling designs and compares several methods, including sampling with replacement, Poisson sampling, and Rao-Sampford sampling.

## Project Contents

- Presentation slides
- Monte Carlo simulations
- Comparison of IPPS sampling methods
- Application to Belgian municipalities data

## Files

- `Slides_Survey_Sampling_IPPS.pdf`: presentation slides explaining the IPPS framework, the associated estimators, the simulation setup, and the empirical comparison of different sampling designs.

## Main Methods

- IPPSWR
- Hansen-Hurwitz estimator
- IPPSWOR Poisson sampling
- Horvitz-Thompson estimator
- Rao-Sampford sampling
- Monte Carlo comparison with SRSWOR and Bernoulli designs
